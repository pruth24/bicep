B.I.C.E.P

Videography


We are thrilled to announce an exhilarating opportunity for all talented video editors out there! BICEP is hosting a Video Editing Competition that promises to be an exhilarating showcase of creativity, skill, and storytelling prowess. Join us for an adrenaline-filled day of creativity and camaraderie with fellow video editing enthusiasts.

Selection process:
 1. The submissions will be reviewed by a panel of judges consisting of college faculty and experts in the field.
2. The judging criteria will include creativity, originality, relevance to the theme, visual impact, and adaptability.
3. The winning video will be chosen based on the judges' decision, and the winner will be notified via email or phone.

Prizes:
 1. The winner will receive ₹3000 cash prize

2. The runner-up will receive ₹1500 cash prize

Don't miss this incredible opportunity to showcase your talent and contribute to the visual identity of BICEP. We look forward to receiving your creative and innovative videos.

Best of luck to all participants!
