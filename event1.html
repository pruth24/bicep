B.I.C.E.P

Logo Design Competetion


All creative minds and talented designers! BMSIT&M is thrilled to announce a logo competition for BICEP [BMSIT Innovation Center and Entrepreneurship Park]. This is your chance to showcase your artistic skills and leave a lasting mark on our prestigious institution. BICEP is a center dedicated to fostering innovation, encouraging entrepreneurship, and nurturing the next generation of leaders. We believe that a captivating logo will reflect the essence of our mission and values. We invite all students, faculty, and staff to participate in this exciting competition.

Here are the guidelines for the logo design:
 1. Theme: The logo should represent the spirit of innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration. It should be modern, dynamic, and visually appealing.
2. Colors: Feel free to explore a wide range of colors, but keep in mind that the logo should be versatile and work well in both color and grayscale.
3. Typography: Choose a font that complements the design and conveys a sense of professionalism.
4. Simplicity: The logo should be simple and easily recognizable, even when scaled down or used in small sizes.
5. Originality: Ensure that your design is original and does not infringe upon any copyrights or trademarks.

Submission guidelines:
 1. Deadline and further details will notified soon.
2. Format: Submit your entries in digital format (JPEG or PNG)
3. Multiple Submissions: Participants may submit multiple entries, but each entry should be submitted separately.
4. Include Details: In your submission email or form, include your full name, contact information, and a brief explanation of the concept behind your logo.

Selection process:
 1. The submissions will be reviewed by a panel of judges consisting of college faculty and experts in the field.
2. The judging criteria will include creativity, originality, relevance to the theme, visual impact, and adaptability.
3. The winning logo will be chosen based on the judges' decision, and the winner will be notified via email or phone.

Prizes:
 1. The winner will receive ₹2000 cash prize and the runner-up will receive ₹1000 cash prize
2. The winning logo will be officially adopted as the logo for BICEP and will be used in all relevant promotional materials and platforms.
Don't miss this incredible opportunity to showcase your talent and contribute to the visual identity of BICEP. We look forward to receiving your creative and innovative logo designs. Best of luck to all participants!
